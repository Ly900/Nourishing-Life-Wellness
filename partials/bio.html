Khanh Nguyen

M.OM, L.Ac., Licensed Acupuncturist, Chinese Herbalist

Khanh received her Masters in Oriental Medicine from the Maryland University of Integrative Health, formerly Tai Sophia Institute. She plans to complete her doctorate degree by December 2016.

Khanh is humble, generous, and has an incredible work ethic. She skillfully applies what she knows to help each patient heal. Khanh is experienced in Five Element Acupuncture, Traditional Chinese Medicine, and Chinese Herbal Medicine. She has helped patients with low back pain, sciatica, diabetes, irregular periods, infertility, allergies, knee pain, depression, anxiety, fibromyalgia, and much more.
